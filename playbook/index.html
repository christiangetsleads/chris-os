Guardrails

Three rules that keep the ratio honest.

RULE 01

When in doubt, post lead gen.

If you are not sure what a piece is, it defaults to authority. The spine is allowed to flex up. The supporting pillars are not.

RULE 02

Never two soft posts back to back.

Faith, fitness, and family pieces get spaced out. A business owner should never open your page and see a lifestyle feed.

RULE 03

Every soft post ties back to the work.

Faith means integrity in delivery. Fitness means discipline in execution. Family means you do not ghost. Always land the plane on why they should trust you with their budget.
